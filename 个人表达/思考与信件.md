# 思考与信件

## 给朋友的信

I hope this email finds you well.

It appears that the girl, who misplaced her headphones, her sunbonnet, and nearly her phone, neglected to bring her pen this afternoon. However, I think it is okay. After all, there is much to express, and the pen is just one of many tools.[...]

The reason she pops out could be attributed to the striking and transformative television dramas and films she has indulged in—whether for sheer entertainment or educational purposes—which have evidently left quite an impression on her. But more importantly, she learned how to appreciate silence, how to sit in a room without any noise, and how to let her thoughts wander freely.[...]

She discovered a newfound appreciation for uninterrupted long takes in both film and television. In June, she immersed herself in the "Before" trilogy: *Before Sunrise* (1994), *Before Sunset* (2004), and *Before Midnight* (2013). The trilogy taught her that sometimes the most profound moments happen in ordinary conversations, with simple camera work and genuine dialogues.[...]

Perhaps it is due to her lack of reading and watching, that she ultimately comes to the realization—at the very last moment—that you have discerned her thoughts through certain expressions she herself was barely aware of. That's a kind of empathy I've never experienced before.[...]

I admire integrity. So I confessed that I liked you before, on and off, for a short time. The feelings I have was suppressed by the attempts I made. They come alive in fleeting moments: when you donned the winter coat, when you smiled at the joke, when you helped carry the bag. But these moments couldn't define a relationship, so I chose silence instead.[...]

I have intended to send it to you until birthday, but I wasn't sure if you will be happy about it *-*.

Hope you can enjoy the brand new campus life. [cheers!]

## 个人思考与观察

### 关于成长与选择

人不是被过去决定的，而是被未来召唤。

那些曲折，终会成为养分。

任何人，不管是生存还是创造，都是主观为自我，客观为别人。就像太阳发光，首先是自己生存运动的必然现象，照耀万物，不过是它派生的一种客观结果。[...]

### 对自我的理解

想要什么？酷和好玩还是内心平静。

共情力，想象力解释世界，看到动机，理解作品，理解复杂的东西。

允许感受切片——感受是我自己的还是别人的？

当下不舒服直接走，保护自己能量的理由。
